About Us

Shakti Laundry has proudly served Rajkot with exceptional laundry services for over 10 years. Our dedication to cleanliness, prompt service, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the community. Whether it’s daily wear, office attire, or special garments, we treat each item with care.

We offer a full range of laundry solutions including wash & fold, ironing, dry cleaning, and express delivery. Our modern machines and eco-friendly detergents ensure deep cleaning while protecting fabric quality. We also provide convenient pickup and delivery services to save you time and hassle.

Located at Jagannath Chowk in Anantanagar, we’re easy to find and ready to serve. At Shakti Laundry, your clothes are in good hands — washed, dried, ironed, and returned looking fresh and crisp.
